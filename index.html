Tapiko

Uses an iPhone's NFC capabilities to read Japanese PASMO and Suica transit cards and display their balance and transaction history.

The application does not write to cards or perform payments. It only reads card information and displays the stored balance and transaction history. It cannot modify, recharge, or otherwise change any PASMO or Suica cards.

The app provides the following functionality: Supports both PASMO and Suica cards. Displays the current card balance and balance history. Displays the last 10 transactions and identifies how the card was used, such as train, bus, retail purchase, or recharge. Processes card data entirely on-device using native iPhone NFC. Supports both English and Japanese languages. Supports multiple cards. Displays a chart of card charges by month in a year, over multiple years.

Privacy Policy

This application reads balance and transaction history from compatible PASMO and Suica transit cards using Apple's Core NFC framework.

Tapiko is a read-only application. It cannot modify, recharge, or otherwise change any PASMO or Suica card.

Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

Tapiko App does not:

  • Collect personal information.
  • Collect location information.
  • Collect analytics or tracking data.
  • Contain in-app purchases.
  • Display advertisements.
  • Require login credentials.
  • Request credit card information.
  • Require subscriptions.
  • Transmit card data to external servers.

No personal information is collected, stored, transmitted, or shared.

All card reading is performed locally on the user's device.

Tapiko requires access to the iPhone's NFC reader in order to scan compatible PASMO and Suica cards.
